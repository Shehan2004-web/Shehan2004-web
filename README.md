## 🏆 Signature Projects

<details>
<summary><b>🎓 ZisuGen — AI Student Community (CSR · EdTech)</b></summary>
<br>

> *"Thousands of students lost access to study materials due to floods and economic hardships."*

**Challenge:** No centralized way to connect donors with students in need or provide instant academic support.

**Solution Built:**
- 🤖 Syllabus-trained **AI Tutor** (Gemini AI) — 24/7 free academic support
- 🗺️ Real-time **Urgent Stories Donation Map** — connects donors to students
- 🔬 **10+ Interactive Virtual Labs** — science, math, programming
- 🔄 **Auto-matching** system for donors ↔ students

**Stack:** `Next.js` `Firebase` `Gemini AI` `Google Maps API`
**Impact:** `500+ Active Students` · `10+ Virtual Labs` · `24/7 AI Support`

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>🍔 Food Delivery App — 3-way Real-Time Platform</b></summary>
<br>

**Challenge:** Orchestrating real-time sync between customers, restaurants, and riders with sub-second latency.

**Solution:** Cloud-native React Native app with Firebase Firestore real-time sync, Google Maps route optimization, automated order dispatching.

**Stack:** `React Native` `Firebase` `Google Maps API`
**Impact:** `5k+ Downloads` · `4.8 Star Rating`

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>📚 Smart Hybrid Learning Ecosystem — LMS Platform</b></summary>
<br>

**Challenge:** Managing growing student base across online & physical channels — manual bank slip verification, no delivery tracking.

**Solution:** API-driven LMS with automated payment verification (local banks), real-time logistics tracking, secure online exam portal.

**Stack:** `Custom REST API` `MySQL` `JavaScript`
**Impact:** `100% Auto-Verification` · `60% Less Admin Overhead`

</details>
